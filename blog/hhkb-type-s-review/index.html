HHKB Type-S Review

March 1, 2024 in Reviews by Husenjan Hesenjan2 minutes

A review of HHKB Type-S keyboard.

Introduction

Around a year ago, I heard about the HHKB Type-S keyboard and I fell in love with the keyboard. However, it came with a hefty 400$ price tag which led to me not purchasing the keyboard at that time but for a week ago I saw someone selling their HHKB Type-S for 220$ and I instantly purchased it. In this blog post I’ll go through my personal experience with HHKB Type-S keyboard.

HHKB Type-S Function Key Issue

The HHKB Type-S Keyboard has an issue where the function keys (F1, F2, F3, and etc..) is not detected on Macbooks. The issue can be resolved by installing Karabiner Elements and once it’s installed you will need to reboot your device.

HHKB Type-S Experience

Here’s a overview of my weekly experience with HHKB keyboard.

  • Week 1: Typing with HHKB Type-S feels fantastic and my Word-Per-Minute (WPM) is around 70 WPM to 80 WPM.
  • Week 2: Typing with HHKB Type-s still feels fantastic and my Word-Per-Minute (WPM) is around 80 to 100 WPM.
  • Week 3: The HHKB Type-S has increased my productivity significantly as I’m able to switch between devices to multi-task.

Gaming With HHKB Type-S

The HHKB Type-S keyboard is fantastic for programming, writing emails, writing blogs and switching between multiple of devices. However, I wouldn’t recommend the HHKB Type-S keyboard for gaming as the CONTROL Key is placed where the CAPS Key is located and that can be difficult to get used to. If you’re searching for a gaming keyboard I would highly recommend Wooting 80HE which is getting released at 2024 June.

Conclusion

The HHKB Type-S is a light-weight and compatible keyboard which comes with all the features which you need for an office environment. I would recommend the keyboard for people who spends most of their time programming, writing emails, and writing blogs. I wouldn’t recommend the keyboard for gamers since there are better options such as Wooting 80HE.
